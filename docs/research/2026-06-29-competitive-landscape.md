## Part I — App-by-app (by cluster)

### A. Daily-connection / questions
- **Lovewick** — "Couple Questions, Date Ideas." Explicitly **anti-quiz/anti-shame** (positions against "competitive quizzes that shame you"). 1,000+ therapist-co-designed cards + a "Memories" timeline. Plus: **$9.99/mo, $14.99/quarter, $29.99/yr.** 4.8★ (~1.9K). 💢 Backlash when free daily questions cut 10/day→1/day at paywall launch.
- **Flamme** (rebrand of "Sparks," 2023) — "daily relationship rituals," proactive-wellness framing. 1,500+ questions + games + date tracker + **AI Love Coach** (2025: Duo AI, LDR Buddy, AI Date Planner). Weekly $6.99 / Monthly $12.99 / Annual $44.99. 4.7★ (~408). 💢 Onboarding assumes your relationship has problems — "remedial rather than celebratory."
- **Agapé** — ⚠️ **NOT an AI coach** (brief was wrong); only "AI" is ML question-selection. One personalised Q/day, both answer then unlock. From 20+ yrs research (Dr. Ron Rogge). **"Daily Agapé" SMS version.** YC **S21** (not 2024–26); ~$2M raised. 4.8★ (16.4K). 💢 paywall friction, repetitive.
- **Sparkz** — ⚠️ **NOT an AI coach**; a couples bucket-list app (~Apr 2026). Thin web presence, TikTok/IG-driven. Pricing `[UNVERIFIED]`.
- **Nustawave** — 🚫 **no verifiable existence** as of Jun 2026. Recommend re-checking the spelling/source.

### B. The Asia giant
- **Between** (VCNC 2011 → **Krafton-owned since 2021**) — the dominant Asian couples app. Private 2-person space (chat, Memory Box, calendar, anniversaries), monetised via **stickers + storage**, ad-free. Trajectory: 2.35M (2013) → Japan's #1 SNS → 10M downloads (2014) → ~35–40M lifetime claimed; **Thailand/Malaysia/Singapore consistently top-10 markets.** Now ~"Between Plus" $2.99/mo–$26.99 lifetime. **Strategic lesson:** dominance came from **cultural fit**, but it still got spun out/sold and went into strategic neglect. ⚠️ Myth-correction: the 2023 layoffs were at VCNC's **Tada** ride-hailing arm, not a Between shutdown. Current SEA usage is **legacy/unverified.**

### C. Logistics / calendar / finance
- **Cupla** — shared calendar for couples + "Cupla Assist" AI co-pilot. Free / $4.99/mo / $34.99/yr (A/B to $7.99/$59.99). 1M+ downloads claimed, 4.7★. 💢 interface feels "busy"/overwhelming.
- **Maple** — ⚠️ a **family/parents** organiser, not a romance app. AI that turns emails into tasks. 4.3★. 💢 confusing per-person billing.
- **Honeydue** — couples finance, **completely free** (ads + tips; debit card discontinued Sept 2023). YC S17; **acquired by Mission Lane (2021)**, now maintenance mode. Per-account privacy toggles are the standout. 💢 **bank-sync is the #1 complaint** (stops updating after 1–2 weeks). **The cautionary tale for any bank-linking feature.**

### D. Therapy / communication / coaching
- **Lasting** (Talkspace-acquired 2020) — marriage-counseling app, self-guided **Gottman-based** sessions. Now **$39.99/mo** (was $11.99). 💢 "used to be cheaper," **hard-to-cancel**; a bug once revealed a partner's answer early.
- **Relish** — coaching with **unlimited 1:1 human coach messaging** (Lesley Eccles, ex-FanDuel). Shifted from $156/mo coaching → "Relish+" ~$3.75/mo. 💢 aggressive upselling; feels stagnant.
- **Evergreen** — "relationship growth… cheaper than therapy." Daily Qs, quizzes, games, gamified. ~$9.99/mo (ladder to $69.99). **4.8★ (~54K)** — very high. 💢 heavy paywall ("you don't get to do much" free).
- **"Official" by Blueheart** — ⚠️ live product is **"Blueheart: Relationship Health"**; a separate "Official" rebrand is `[UNVERIFIED]`. Sextech DNA (Dr. Kat Hertlein) brought into a mainstream couples app. 💢 billing complaints dominate.
- **Gottman Card Decks (free)** — digital conversation flashcards, **no upsell**, the category's **trust anchor** (Lasting & Evergreen both invoke Gottman research). ⚠️ "Gottman Love Notes" is a free **newsletter**, not a paid app.

### E. Intimacy / "spicy" / games

## Part II — Synthesis

**Crowded:** daily-questions (Paired owns it), calendar/logistics (commoditised by Google Cal/YNAB), LDR widgets.
**Underserved:** (a) the **affordable middle of couples therapy** — *"There's no Calm for couples"*; (b) **conflict-navigation** (therapists note apps "avoid conflict resolution entirely"); (c) **how two partners' patterns interact.**

**The AI-coach wave:** what's working = **live voice mediation** ("AI listens in on a real fight," called "insane") + **clinical-method grounding** (Gottman/EFT + licensed-therapist provenance) + accessibility. What's gimmicky = price-to-value mismatch, generic advice, the **APA headwind** (Nov 2025 advisory: AI wellness apps "should not substitute for a professional"), the **"triangulation" critique** (AI-as-mediator "never forces them to deal with the issue with the person"), sycophancy, privacy (not HIPAA-bound). **Defensible framing: "AI-as-coach-*between*-sessions," not "AI-as-therapist-*during*-conflict."**

**White space (what NO app does well):** the **asymmetric/solo-usage** problem (#1 structural churn driver); too-shallow-or-too-gimmicky; the **scorekeeping trap** ("an app that scores… creates a transactional frame toxic to intimacy" — ⚠️ directly relevant to our wavelength score); ignoring conflict; **billing dark patterns** (#1 universal hate); **reliability** (Kindu/Honeydue both concept-right, execution-fatal).

**Pricing benchmarks:** daily-connection cluster ~**$30–45/yr**; therapy/coaching **2–4× more** ($39.99/mo Lasting, $24.95/mo CoupleWork); Asia (Between) far cheaper ($2.99/mo, $26.99 lifetime). Free-only (Honeydue, Gottman) is loved but not independently durable. **No tier-1 tracker publishes a couples-app category report — all market-size numbers are `[UNVERIFIED]`.**
