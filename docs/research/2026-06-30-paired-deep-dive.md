## 2. Pricing & billing

- **USD $14.99/mo · $74.99/yr** (~$6.25/mo annualised). **UK £14.99/mo · £39.96/yr.** No verified lifetime tier. **No PPP — auto-FX**, no dedicated SGD/SEA pricing.

## 3. Content & editorial engine

- **In-house expert:** Moraya Seeger DeGeare, LMFT (EFT-certified). **16+ therapist/academic panel** (Pepper Schwartz, Terri Orbuch, Marisa Cohen, Jacqui Gabb, et al.).
- **Volume:** **128 question packs** (6–11 Qs each) + quizzes/games; **8 themes** (Appreciation, Nostalgia, Play, Adventure, Values & Dreams, Family & Friends, Communication, Sex & Romance).
- **Grounding:** Gottman (bids, Love Maps, 5:1), EFT, attachment, love languages, and Gabb's *Enduring Love?* (everyday small gestures). Per-question peer-reviewed citations.
- **Journeys:** 5–10 session guided courses + produced expert video.
- **Refresh cadence: not disclosed** ("continually added"); users still report repetition/running-dry. **Personalisation is demographic + couple-driven, NOT predictively adaptive** — the static-library + no-real-adaptation gap is real (our AI-prompt opening holds).

## 4. Emotional design philosophy

- **Stance:** warm/playful, **preventative** ("toothbrush not dentist"; "gym for your relationship"), for couples *already in love* — explicitly **not** crisis/conflict.
- **Anti-judgment by design:** **no scores, no grades, no leaderboards, no pass/fail.** Answers = "opportunities for discussion." → *strong external validation of our wavelength-reframe constraint (R3 / STRATEGY §4.3).*
- **Reveal logic:** hide-until-both = anticipation + authentic independent answers + rediscovered curiosity ("I didn't know you felt that way"). Same engine we have.
- **"Neutral intermediary":** the app raising hard topics (money/sex) feels safer than one partner nagging — *useful framing for our Money Dates / Journeys.*
- **Intensity ramps gradually**; tone conversational not clinical.
- **Criticisms:** prompts sometimes feel **clinical / like homework**; **no support at the moment incompatibility surfaces** (reviewers call it "Un-paired"); **no explicit inclusivity statement** (LGBTQ+ felt-included but unstated); assumes baseline psychological safety.

## 5. Notifications & habit mechanics

- **3 notification moments:** daily prompt (each partner's **own set time**), **"your partner answered,"** and premium **"nudge."** Tone = low-intensity/social, not engagement-spammy. (Users report notifications are **glitchy**; want "remind only if not yet answered.")
- **24h expiry = loss aversion without a visible streak whip** (free loses the Q; premium gets archive). Streaks exist but **subtle** (Us tab); **nudge** = dyadic streak protection.
- **Reciprocal loop:** "Discuss" tab unlocks only when both answer → answering is rewarded with partner's thoughts; couples build a scheduled ritual.
- **LDR-ready:** per-partner timezone-aware delivery + async unlock (relevant to our JB–SG causeway angle).
- **Retention (peer-reviewed, JMIR 2025):** +36% relationship quality over 3mo; **64.3%** "stronger" at 1mo; **heavy users (6–7 d/wk) 70.6% vs 54.1%** light. **Amplitude: Day-0 optimisation drove their biggest engagement lift.**
- **Habit philosophy:** "5 min/day" micro-ritual; BJ-Fogg-aligned (M/A/P) though never name-checked; Memrise behavioural-design lineage.
