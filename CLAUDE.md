## Workflow

- After 2 failed attempts at the same approach, stop and rethink — don't brute-force.
- Verify before claiming done — run tests/commands and show the output.
- Delegate verbose operations (test runs, log analysis) to subagents to preserve context.
- Follow the rules in `.claude/rules/`.

## Do NOT

- Don't claim something works without running it.
- Don't silence errors with a broad try/catch — find the root cause.
- Don't commit secrets (`.env`, `*.pem`, `*.key`, credentials) — a hook blocks writes to these.
